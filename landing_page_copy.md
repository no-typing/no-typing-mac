# No-Typing: Universal Voice Dictation

**Core Pitch**: A lightning-fast, privacy-first AI voice dictation app. Speak naturally and type instantly into any application, form, or document with unparalleled accuracy. (Currently available for macOS; coming soon to Windows, Linux, Android, and iOS).

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. 100% Local & Private AI
- **On-Device Whisper Models**: Powered by state-of-the-art AI running locally. Optimized for Apple Silicon for blazing-fast performance on Mac, with optimized binaries coming for desktop and mobile architectures.
- **Absolute Privacy**: No audio recordings are ever sent to the cloud. What you say stays on your device.
- **Offline Capable**: Works perfectly even when you don't have an internet connection.

### 2. Magic Actions ✨ (Automations)
- **Voice Commands**: Control your device with your voice. Speak exact trigger words rather than typing them:
  - Say *"Submit"* or *"Send"* to instantly press `Return`/`Enter`.
  - Say *"Next"* to press `Tab` and jump between form fields.
  - Say *"Clear"* to automatically select all text and delete it.
  - Say *"Undo"* or *"Oops"* to trigger system undo (`Cmd+Z` / `Ctrl+Z`) and erase a mistake.
- **Smart Snippets**: Custom text replacements. Define shorthand triggers (e.g., "my email") that automatically expand into full phrases or correct uniquely spelled names on the fly.

### 3. Universal Media Transcription
- **Local File Uploads**: Drag and drop any offline audio or video file into the app to generate a clean, readable text transcript in seconds.
- **Social Link Ingestion**: Paste a YouTube, TikTok, or Vimeo link. The app instantly fetches the thumbnail, downloads the audio in the background, and transcribes the entire video offline.

### 4. Intelligent Text Processing
- **AI Cleaning Pipeline**: Optional advanced formatting that uses Foundation Models to fix stuttering, remove filler words ("um", "uh"), and perfect the grammar before inserting the text.
- **Auto-Punctuation**: Automatically adds periods, commas, and question marks where appropriate so you don't have to awkwardly say "comma" or "period" out loud.

### 5. Productivity Dashboard & History
- **Searchable Archive**: A beautifully redesigned, paginated table storing your entire transcription history.
- **Data Insights**: Track your productivity natively with built-in metrics calculating your total "Words Spoken" and estimated "Time Saved" vs. typing manually.

### 6. Seamless OS Integration
- **Global Hotkeys**: Bind a custom keyboard shortcut to start and stop recording instantly, no matter what app you are currently in.
- **Floating HUD**: A sleek, unobtrusive, drag-anywhere recording widget that provides visual feedback without getting in your way.
- **Premium Design**: A stunning, modern UI inspired by top-tier utilities, featuring translucent frosted-glass cards, rich gradients, and native animations.
